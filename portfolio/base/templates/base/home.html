About Me:

Ever since my first laptop I got around the age of 10 I have always enjoyed using computers, whether that was for gaming or just browsing the internet. My first programming experience was probably around the age of 12 when I was modifying minecraft mod files with notepad++ to change how some of the things in the mod worked. I thought it was really cool that I could add my own custom touch to the game and from this point on I new I wanted to be a programmer when I was older. I ended up going to community college for 2 years with intent to transfer to a 4 year university in pursuit of a computer science degree, however once I graduated I realized that I don't need a degree and that I could buy courses online and teach myself how to code. After completing online courses on JavaScript React Js, and Node Js, I was ready to make some projects some of which are listed here on my site and to build a portfolio which would hopefully end up getting me a job.
